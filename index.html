Our Code of Conduct

DreamCraft Projects is committed to upholding the highest standards of ethical and lawful business conduct. We recognise our responsibility to foster fair, safe, and inclusive workplaces while contributing positively to the broader community.

Our Commitments:

Legal Compliance: Adherence to all applicable Commonwealth and NSW legislation, including Fair Work Act 2009, Work Health and Safety Act 2011 (NSW), Modern Slavery Act 2018.
Anti-Slavery and Ethical Labour: We strictly oppose modern slavery, forced labour, and human trafficking. All work must be performed voluntarily with fair compensation.
Fair Treatment: Promoting a work environment free from discrimination, harassment, bullying, or victimisation. All workers treated with dignity and respect.
Safe Workplaces: Ensuring all worksites are safe, clean, and meet or exceed WHS standards. Risk management and training are mandatory.
Integrity: Acting honestly and transparently. Avoiding conflicts of interest, bribery, or corruption.
Environmental Stewardship: Operating with care for the environment in line with our Environmental Policy and NSW environmental laws.
Continuous Improvement: Regularly reviewing performance, encouraging feedback, and committing to continuous development.
